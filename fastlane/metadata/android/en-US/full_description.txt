Image to PDF Maker is an open-source, privacy-first application designed to convert your photos and images into a single PDF document quickly and securely.

Key Features:
- Ad-Free Experience: No interruptions, just functionality.
- Privacy-Centric: Operates entirely offline with no internet permission required. Your data never leaves your device.
- Intuitive Organization: Use drag-and-drop to reorder your images exactly how you want them.
- Flexible Quality: Choose between Compact, Standard, or Best quality to balance file size and resolution.
- Multi-Language Support: Available in over 20 languages.
- Modern Design: Built using Jetpack Compose and Material 3 for a smooth, native Android experience.

Simple, honest, and functional.
